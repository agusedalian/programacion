ITINERARIOS POR EL MUNDO

Indonesia: Explore the stunning diversity of Indonesia by starting in Bali, where you can enjoy its beautiful beaches and vibrant culture. Then, head to Yogyakarta to visit the ancient temples of Borobudur and Prambanan. Continue your journey to Komodo Island to see the famous Komodo dragons and dive in the crystal-clear waters. Finally, experience the rich culture of Ubud before departing.

Francia: Begin your journey in Paris, the "City of Love," and visit iconic landmarks such as the Eiffel Tower and Louvre Museum. Explore the picturesque Provence region with its lavender fields and historic towns. Travel to the French Riviera for glamorous beaches and charming towns like Nice and Cannes. Conclude your trip with wine tasting in Bordeaux and a visit to the stunning Mont Saint-Michel.

Paises bajos: Start your Dutch adventure in Amsterdam, where you can explore its historic canals, museums, and vibrant neighborhoods. Next, visit Zaanse Schans to see traditional windmills and picturesque Dutch villages. Travel to Keukenhof to witness the mesmerizing tulip fields (in spring). End your journey in Rotterdam for modern architecture and art.

Italia: Kick off your Italian exploration in Rome, where you can visit the Colosseum, Roman Forum, and Vatican City. Head to Florence for Renaissance art and architecture. Continue to Venice for a gondola ride and picturesque canals. Explore the Amalfi Coast's stunning coastline, and finish with a trip to the historic city of Pompeii.

Suiza: Commence your Swiss adventure in Zurich, then journey to Lucerne for its charming old town and stunning lake. Explore the Jungfrau region with its breathtaking alpine landscapes. Visit Interlaken for adventure sports and then proceed to Geneva for its international charm and beautiful lakeside.

Austria: Begin in Vienna, Austria's capital, and explore its imperial palaces and museums. Travel to Salzburg, the birthplace of Mozart and a city of stunning baroque architecture. Enjoy the Austrian Alps in Innsbruck and go hiking or skiing, and end your journey in the charming city of Graz.

Grecia: Embark on a Greek odyssey by starting in Athens, home to the Acropolis and ancient history. Head to Santorini for its breathtaking sunsets and white-washed buildings. Visit the historic city of Thessaloniki and discover the ancient ruins of Delphi. Conclude your trip in Crete, where you can explore the beautiful beaches and rich culture.

Tailandia: Commence your Thai adventure in Bangkok, exploring the grand temples and vibrant street life. Head to Chiang Mai for a taste of Northern Thai culture and visit the Elephant Nature Park. Relax on the stunning beaches of Phuket and explore the nearby Phi Phi Islands. Conclude your journey in the historic city of Ayutthaya.

Republica Chequa: Start your Czech journey in Prague, the "City of a Hundred Spires," and explore its historic Old Town and Prague Castle. Visit the picturesque town of Český Krumlov for its charming old-world charm. Explore the spa town of Karlovy Vary and finish your trip in Brno, known for its vibrant cultural scene.
